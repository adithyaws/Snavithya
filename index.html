Our Premium Services

CCTV Installation

Complete end-to-end CCTV installation and maintenance for homes and commercial businesses. We provide high-quality cameras with night vision, motion tracking, and mobile viewing capabilities to ensure your property is secure 24/7. Our expert technicians handle wiring and DVR/NVR setups efficiently.

House Wiring

Professional electrical house wiring tailored for new constructions or renovations. We ensure total safety from short circuits, handle lighting fixtures, distribution boards, and earth leakage protection. All our work complies with the highest national safety standards.

Computer Networking

Seamlessly connect your digital world with our advanced computer networking solutions. From configuring home Wi-Fi and routers to deploying large-scale office LAN setups, server racks, and VPNs, we guarantee fast, stable, and secure intranet and internet connectivity.

Computer Hardware

Expert diagnosis, repairing, and upgrading of computer desktops and laptops. Whether you need a RAM upgrade, a new solid-state drive (SSD) installed, motherboard repairs, or custom PC builds for gaming and heavy workloads, our technicians provide fast turnarounds.

Software Installations

Get your software running smoothly without the hassle. We offer operating system installations (Windows/Linux/Mac), virus removal, data recovery, and installation of productivity suites and specialized software like Adobe CC, AutoCAD, or accountancy packages.

Plumbing Services

Rapid response plumbing services for your home. We fix leaks, unclog drains, install bathroom and kitchen fittings, change pipelines, and repair water pumps. Our dependable plumbers ensure minimal disruption to your daily life with clean and efficient work.

Solar System Setup

Harvest clean, renewable energy with our cutting-edge solar installations. We handle everything from assessing your roof's capacity, installing panels and inverters, to connecting you to the main grid. Cut down your electricity bills significantly with an eco-friendly solar solution.

TV & Radio Repairing

Breathe new life into your electronics! Our specialized technicians repair LED, LCD, OLED TVs, and complex radio systems. From fixing distorted screens and broken audio channels to motherboard circuitry level repairs, we save you money on buying new replacements.
